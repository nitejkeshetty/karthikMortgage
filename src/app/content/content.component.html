The Path Ahead


1.

What's the budget? Let's get started!

Dreaming of owning your dream home? Before you dive into making offers or spending sunny days touring countless properties, connect with us first. We’ll help you determine your budget and guide you through the onboarding process. Using our affordability checker, we can secure a Decision in Principle (DIP) or an Agreement in Principle (AIP) for you to present to estate agents.


2.

Make your offer and seal the deal!

Discovered your dream home within budget? Perfect location—near the schools for your kids, a quick commute to work, and close to your favorite spots for evening dog walks. Reach out to us right away, and we’ll help transform your offer into a mortgage application, complete with personalized advice, ideal terms, and expert guidance!


3.

Under the Surface:

Before the official mortgage offer is issued by your chosen lender, they will conduct a valuation and survey to meet their requirements. You should do the same! We always recommend a survey to identify any potential hidden issues. Rest assured, we're here to guide and support you every step of the way.


4.

Offer Secured! Let the solicitors weave their expertise!

The legal process is the final and most vital stage. The solicitors must uncover any potential legal concerns to ensure all parties are fully informed and in agreement with the terms and conditions of the mortgage.


5.

Finalizing the Deal

We're here to support you every step of the way, driving the process forward until the moment you can finally grab those keys! Together, we'll make your journey smooth and successful.
